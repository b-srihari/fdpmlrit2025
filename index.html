About MLR Institute of Technology

Established Excellence

Founded in 2005 by KMR Education Trust, located at Dundigal, Hyderabad, Telangana. Committed to integrated curriculum with equal importance to academics and employable skills.

Accreditation

Academic Programs

Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Program Overview

The B.Tech program in Computer Science and Engineering was started in 2005 with an intake of 60 students, which has grown significantly to 420 students in 2024. The department also offers M.Tech program since 2011 with an intake of 18 students.

The B.Tech CSE program has been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) since 2008, demonstrating our commitment to quality education and continuous improvement.

Program Objectives

Primary Objectives

This Faculty Development Program focuses on fundamental concepts and techniques related to Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Big Data, IoT and Data Science. The primary objective is to enrich faculty members of Computer Science stream with preliminaries of artificial intelligence and its modern applications.

The program addresses modern trends in the field of Artificial Intelligence with real-time problem solving, focusing on hands-on implementation of various algorithms to deliver practical skills to participants.

Key Focus Areas

  • Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • Data Science & Big Data Analytics
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Digital Image Processing
  • Hands-on Algorithm Implementation
  • Real-time Problem Solving

Program Topics

Artificial Intelligence and Applications
Introduction to Learning Techniques with Supervised Learning using Python
Various Unsupervised Learning Techniques with Clustering
